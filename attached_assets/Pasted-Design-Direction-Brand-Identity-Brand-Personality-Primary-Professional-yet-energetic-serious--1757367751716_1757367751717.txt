🎯 Specific Design Recommendations:
Answer to Agent's Questions:
1. Brand Direction:
Professional with momentum energy - Think "Bloomberg Terminal meets modern SaaS" but approachable for small business owners.
2. User Priorities:

Business owners (viewing SMS and dashboard) - 60% priority
Team members (data entry) - 25% priority
Admins (setup and configuration) - 15% priority

3. Mobile Usage:
Critical - Dashboard must be fully functional on mobile. Business owners check metrics on phones constantly.
4. Data Density:
Focused displays - Each KPI gets breathing room. Better to scroll than overwhelm.
5. Visual Style:
Inspiration: Linear, Stripe Dashboard, Notion - clean, modern, data-focused but not sterile.

Key Design Patterns:

Progressive disclosure - Show basics first, expand on demand
Contextual help - Tooltips explaining KPI meanings
Status indicators - Clear success/error states
Loading states - Skeleton screens during data fetching
Empty states - Helpful guidance when no data exists
